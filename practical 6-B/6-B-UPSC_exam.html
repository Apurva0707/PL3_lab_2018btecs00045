Applicants should note that there will be a ceiling on the number of candidates allotted to each of the Centres except Ahmedabad, Delhi, Dispur and Kolkata. Allotment of Centres will be on the 'first-apply-first allot' basis, and once the capacity of a particular Centre is attained, the same will be frozen. Applicants are, thus, advised that they may apply early so that they could get a Centre of their choice.
